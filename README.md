Interview Preparation Bot 🎯

An AI-powered interview prep tool that reads your resume and generates personalized interview questions based on your actual skills, projects, experience, and education — complete with model answers, difficulty levels, and feedback on your responses.

Upload a resume → get interviewed on exactly what's in it.

How it works

pload your resume (PDF).
The app extracts the text and sends it to Gemini, which pulls out structured info: skills, projects, experience, and education.
Gemini uses that structured info to generate interview questions tailored to your resume, each tagged with a difficulty level and paired with a model answer.
You answer each question in the app.
Gemini compares your answer to the model answer and gives you a score and feedback.

No database, no login — everything runs in a single session.

Features
📄 Resume upload and automatic text extraction
🧠 AI-generated questions based on your specific skills and projects (not generic questions)
🎚️ Difficulty levels (Easy / Medium / Hard)
✅ Model answers for every question
💬 Instant feedback and scoring on your own answers


Tech Stack


Python
Streamlit — web UI
Gemini API — question generation, model answers, feedback
pdfplumber — resume text extraction
